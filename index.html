About me

I am a passionate IT graduate from a small village next to Piła city, located in the northern part of Poland. I've got interested in computers in 2020 when I had to choose my high school. I chose IT school, because I thought that this was the best option for me. As a child, I always loved to know how different pieces of electronics work. Today, I am proud IT graduate and with knowledge I got from my teachers I can finally do what I wanted to do - repair computers.
